Clojure Monger

Monger a modern well maintained Clojure client for MongoDB. It offers support for MongoDB 2.2+ features, has very powerful and expressive query DSL, integrates with libraries like clojure.data.json and JodaTime, makes working with JavaScript map/reduce easier and more.
